Who We Are

Milton Cricket Champs (MCC) is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to building a strong, inclusive cricket community and growing the sport across the Greater Toronto Area. Founded in 2022, we have grown to 70+ members competing in 15+ leagues across multiple formats.
