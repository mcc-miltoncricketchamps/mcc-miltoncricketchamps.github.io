About MCC

Our story, mission, and the cricket community we’re building

Our Story

Milton Cricket Champs (MCC) was founded in 2022 with a simple yet powerful vision — to bring cricket lovers together and build a strong, inclusive community across the Greater Toronto Area.

What began as weekend friendly games among a small group of passionate cricketers has evolved steadily — from local leagues to competing in major tournaments — reflecting our journey of commitment, growth, and love for the game.

Today, MCC is a registered non-profit organization with over 70 active members, united by team spirit and a shared passion for cricket.

At MCC, there is a place for everyone — whether you’re looking to enjoy casual weekend cricket or compete in structured leagues and tournaments. We provide opportunities for players at all levels, while also supporting those who want to develop their skills, compete, and grow.

Over the past few years, MCC has participated in 15+ leagues across multiple formats, securing several championships and establishing itself as one of the most competitive and community-driven cricket clubs in the region.

Beyond the game, MCC is about connection, growth, and opportunity — creating a platform where players come together, compete, and build lasting relationships.

As a registered non-profit, every dollar we raise goes directly back into the sport — supporting equipment, league participation, training facilities, and community-driven initiatives.

MCC continues to grow with a focus on expanding opportunities, supporting grassroots cricket, and strengthening the cricketing community across the GTA.
